Most Recent Reviews

Made

A Swingers wanna be. Unnecessary film in my mind. I dont think anyone had their greatest performance here. Like the low-budget comedy gangster film that it is, this can be kept on the shelf, or discarded whenever you so desire.

The Witch

Despite how much I dislike period pieces, I have to give this a good review. Anya Taylor-Joy is phenomenal, along with the rest of the cast. Story is excellent and cinematography is near perfect.

Earthquake Bird

I honestly thought this was excellent. I dont agree with the mixed reviews that tend to lean negative. Vikander was phenomenal, as usual. The visuals were stunning and the story was captivating. The only thing I will say is maybe some of the supporting roles were incorrectly casted? I dont know. I dont see the negative. Maybe I am too blinded by my never-ending praise for Vikander.
